UNSW Competitive Programming and Mathematics Society aims to foster a community of students who are interested in competitive programming and/or competitive mathematics.

We aim to host regular competitions and workshops to challenge students and improve their problem solving skills.

Everyone is welcome! To stay up to date, join us in these places:

  • SpArc
  • Facebook page
  • Facebook group
  • Discord


Our Competitive Programming Workshops will run on Thursdays 5-7pm in Term 1 Weeks 3, 5, and 7.

Our Competitive Mathematics Workshops will run on Thursdays 6:30-8:30pm in Term 1 Weeks 4, 6, and 8 (Facebook event not created yet).
